# 🧠 AI Medical Report Analyzer

> Turning complex medical reports into simple, actionable insights.

---

## 🚀 Overview

**AI Medical Report Analyzer** is a smart AI-powered web app that helps users understand their medical reports instantly.

Upload a **PDF or image**, and the system will:

* Detect medical parameters (RBC, WBC, Hemoglobin, etc.)
* Identify abnormal values (low/high)
* Explain what it means in simple language
* Suggest possible causes
* Provide practical solutions & precautions

---

## ❗ Problem

Medical reports are difficult to understand for most people.

* Complex medical terminology
* No explanation of abnormal values
* People ignore critical health warnings
* Lack of awareness leads to serious health risks

---

## 💡 Solution

Our AI simplifies everything:

➡ Upload report → Get instant insights

* Highlights abnormal values
* Explains each parameter clearly
* Tells **why it might be abnormal**
* Suggests **what you can do next**

---

## ⚙️ How It Works

1. Upload medical report (PDF/Image)
2. Extract text using OCR
3. Identify key medical parameters
4. Compare with standard ranges
5. Generate AI-powered insights

---

## 🔍 Example Analysis

**Parameter:** RBC Count

* **Your Value:** Low
* **Normal Range:** 4.5 – 5.9 million cells/mcL

**Possible Reasons:**

* Iron deficiency
* Blood loss
* Vitamin deficiency

**Suggested Solutions:**

* Iron-rich diet (spinach, dates, etc.)
* Regular checkups
* Consult a doctor if severe
